# Code of Conduct

This project expects respectful, focused, and constructive collaboration.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful in technical and non-technical discussions.
- Critique ideas and implementations, not people.
- Share context, evidence, and reproduction details when reporting problems.
- Accept feedback and corrections in good faith.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory or hateful language
- Doxxing or sharing private information without consent
- Repeated bad-faith disruption of issues, discussions, or pull requests

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content that violates these expectations. Repeated or severe violations can lead to temporary or permanent bans from project spaces.

## Reporting

Use the process in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) when private handling is needed. For general conduct concerns, open a GitHub issue requesting moderator attention without including sensitive personal details.
